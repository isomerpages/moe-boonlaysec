<p>In BLS Malay Dance Club, we strive to ensure all our members are stretched
to meet their maximum potential. As a dance family, we emphasize teamwork
and bond amongst our members.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/MD1.jpg">
</div>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/MD22.jpg">
</div>
<p>By instilling discipline and resilience in our members, our programmes
are designed to cultivate confidence and commitment in achieving their
goals as performers.. Our dancers have numerous opportunities to perform
and compete in various showcases such as the Esplanade MUARA festival and
the Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation or SYF Celebrates.</p>
<p>These experiences help them develop greater self-confidence and mastery
of their dance skills.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/MD33.jpg">
</div>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/MD43.jpg">
</div>
<p>In developing our leaders, we also find opportunities to send them for
leadership workshops such as Dance Amplified! for Secondary 3 leaders and
developing their talents through Strategic Partnership Co-Curricular Activity
(SP-CCA) for Secondary 1 and 2 students, both organised by MOE CCA Branch.
We also offer attachment programmes to dance companies for industrial experiences.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/MD6.jpg">

<p>Our CCA welcomes dancers of all skill levels and backgrounds to join the
club, promoting the spirit of learning from one another, appreciating our
unique differences and developing individual competencies.</p>
