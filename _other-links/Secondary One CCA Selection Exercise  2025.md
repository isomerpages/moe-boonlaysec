<p>Students will be given a total of 8 choices and are required to select
2 CCAs from each of the CCA type (Clubs &amp; Societies, Performing Arts,
Physical Sports, Uniformed Groups).</p>
<p>Students are to rank their choices in the order of preference. More details
of the individual CCAs can be found on the school website via <a href="https://www.boonlaysec.moe.edu.sg/co-curricular-activities/ccafamilies/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">OUR CCAS</a> or
in the CCA passport distributed during the CCA Experience Day.</p>
