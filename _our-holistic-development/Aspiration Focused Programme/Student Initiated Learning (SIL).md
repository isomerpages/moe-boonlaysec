<h4><strong>Programme Outcomes</strong></h4>
<p>Student-Initiated Learning (SIL) empowers Boon Lay Secondary School students
to take ownership of their learning while exploring their passions across
the <strong>DREAMS domains</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>D</strong>igital &amp; Infotech</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>R</strong>elations &amp; Business</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>E</strong>ngineering, Maths &amp; Science</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>A</strong>rts &amp; Humanities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>M</strong>edia &amp; Design</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>S</strong>ports</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Through SIL, students build essential future-ready competencies such as <strong>self-management, collaboration, problem-solving, innovation</strong>,
and <strong>lifelong learning habits</strong>.</p>
<h4><strong>Description of Programme &amp; Key Activities</strong></h4>
<p>SIL offers <strong>three tiers of learning experiences</strong> to cater
to students’ interests, learning preferences, and levels of independence.</p>
<h4><strong>1. School-Curated Independent Learning&nbsp;</strong></h4>
<p>Students complete simple, safe, and engaging school-curated tasks at their
own pace during Home-Based Learning (HBL). Activities are accessible via <strong>SIL MS Teams</strong> and
the <strong>Student Learning Space (SLS)</strong>.</p>
<p>Examples include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><em>Swift Playgrounds with Your iPad</em> (Digital &amp; Infotech)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><em>Digital Science Experiments</em> (Engineering, Maths &amp; Science)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><em>Active Arcade Movement Challenges</em> (Sports)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><em>Music in Singapore</em> (Arts &amp; Humanities)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>These activities nurture curiosity, creativity, and confidence in independent
learning</p>
<hr>
<h4><strong>2. School-Facilitated Peer Activities&nbsp;</strong></h4>
<p>Students may return to school during HBL to use school facilities and
participate in guided, hands-on sessions that strengthen teamwork and practical
skills.</p>
<p>Examples include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Cricut &amp; 3D printing projects in the Design Studio (Media &amp; Design)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>National Digital Explorer on Workforce Documents - Learn and Certify in
the Computer Labs (Digital &amp; Infotech)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sports training sessions such as basketball, badminton, and Captain’s
Ball (Sports)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>These sessions help students deepen their interests while benefiting from
school expertise and equipment.</p>
<hr>
<h4><strong>3. Fully Student-Initiated Peer Activities</strong></h4>
<p>Students independently plan, organise, and carry out activities with their
peers—encouraging leadership, responsibility, and collaboration.</p>
<p>Examples include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Modern dance rehearsals (Relations &amp; Business)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Student-led video production or photography projects (Arts &amp; Humanities)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Outdoor activities such as volleyball or soccer (Sports)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>This tier nurtures student agency and strengthens social-emotional skills.</p>
<hr>
<h4><strong>SIL Digital Showcase</strong></h4>
<p>Each student documents and presents their learning at the annual <strong>SIL Digital Showcase</strong>.
This celebration highlights creativity, personal growth, and peer inspiration
while reinforcing reflection and communication skills. Through SIL, Boon
Lay Secondary School develops well-rounded, self-directed learners who
are ready to thrive in an ever-changing world.</p>
<p><em>SIL is simple, safe, and joyful — a space for every student to pursue their passions.</em>
