<p>Education and Career Guidance (ECG) helps students develop a sense of
purpose in life. By nurturing self-awareness and self-directedness for
lifelong learning, ECG supports the development of a growth mindset, adaptability,
and resilience, enabling students to embrace future opportunities and stay
relevant in a changing world.</p>
